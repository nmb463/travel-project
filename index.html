Why the Pacific Northwest?

The Pacific Northwest, land of adventures

The Pacific Northwest is known for its clean air and water, and is surrounded by mountains, lakes, rivers, and forests. The scenery changes from lush forests to volcanic mountains, crater lakes, and craggy beaches.

Some activities to enjoy include hiking, kayaking, camping, whale watching, skiing, snowboarding, and wine tasting. For city lovers, there are many art museums, gardens, eclectic eateries (esp. food carts), farmers markets, cafes, and bookshops.
